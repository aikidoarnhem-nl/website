## Stichting Sankaku

Stichting Sankaku has been providing aikido classes in Arnhem and Wageningen for many years. The aim of the foundation is to promote aikido. That goes further than just facilitating classes. Namely:

- Taking care of the training of (new) teachers. Sankaku is affiliated with the JBN.
- Raising more awareness of aikido. For example, by giving guest classes (Artez dance academy, etc.) and workshops (Ministry of SZW, Loyalis, etc.).
